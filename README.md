## Hi, I'm Steve! 👋
<img align="right" width="400" src="https://github.com/stevelonny/jujutracer/blob/main/asset/tree_box.png">

Based in Milan, Italy, I'm currently pursuing a Master's degree in Physics @ [UNIMI](https://fisica.unimi.it/it), specializing in Computational Physics, Many-Body Theory, Quantum Systems and Structure of Matter.
My research focuses on the study of strongly correlated quantum systems, particularly in the context of condensed matter physics.

My Bachelor's degree in Physics was completed at UNIMI as well, with a thesis on optimizing a distributed computing code written in C++/MPI for the simulation of quantum systems: _"Efficient parallelization of quantum trajectories with the Open Time-Dependent Variational Monte Carlo algorithm"_.

During my academic life, I have organized various social events for students. In my free time I enjoy some analogue photography, reading and playing volleyball.
